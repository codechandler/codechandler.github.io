Asteroids in Unity (C#)

I took what I learned from my Classic Atari game and gave it a major overhaul in Unity over the summer. In doing so I had the opportunity to learn C#. Building Asteroids in Unity gave me a better understanding of programming game physics and handling object collisions. It was also really fun to build.

Improvements:

  • Better physics
  • Sprite Animation
  • The user can select their own controls to maneuver the spaceship.
